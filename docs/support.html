Required Permissions

Streak Finder requires read-only Apple Health access to:

  • Step Count
  • Exercise Minutes
  • Apple Stand Hour
  • Active Energy Burned
  • Workouts
  • Mindful Minutes
  • Sleep Analysis
  • Distance Walking & Running
  • Flights Climbed
  • Heart Rate

Troubleshooting

  • If Streak Finder shows no data, open the Health app and confirm Streak Finder has permission to read the activity types you care about.
  • If streaks look incorrect, check that your Apple Health data is up to date. Streak Finder reads what HealthKit provides.
  • If widgets or complications look stale, open Streak Finder once to refresh the local cache.
  • If notifications are not appearing, check that you have enabled at-risk reminders in the app settings and that Do Not Disturb is not active at the scheduled time.

Privacy

Streak Finder does not create an account, does not send your data to a server, and does not include analytics, ads, or tracking.
