Fitness Streaks — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

Fitness Streaks is a local-only app. It reads activity history from Apple Health on your device, computes streaks, and displays them. It does not send any of your data off the device.

What we read

  • Steps, exercise minutes, stand time, active energy, distance, flights climbed
  • Workouts, mindful sessions, sleep analysis

All reads are via Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. Fitness Streaks never writes to Apple Health.

What we store

A local cache of daily activity values is stored on your device in an App Group container, so the widgets and watch complications can render without querying HealthKit every time. You can delete it any time by uninstalling the app.

What we send

Nothing. There is no analytics, no advertising, no cloud sync, no third-party SDKs.

Notifications

If you enable the at-risk reminder, Fitness Streaks schedules a local notification at 7pm via UNUserNotificationCenter. It never leaves your device.
