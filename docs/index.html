Streak Finder

Retro vibes. Modern health. Zero effort.

Streak Finder is a signature retro-pixel fitness tracker for iOS and Apple Watch. It automatically scans your Apple Health history to uncover the momentum you've already built, helping you stay consistent without the manual overhead.

Support

Need help or have feedback? We're here to help you keep those streaks alive. You can reach out directly via email at jackwallner@gmail.com.

Privacy First

Your health data is personal. Streak Finder is built with a local-only architecture. No data is ever uploaded to a server, and there are no third-party trackers or analytics. Your streaks stay on your device.
