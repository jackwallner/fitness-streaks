# Product copy — for tone calibration

Use this only to match voice. Do **not** copy these phrases verbatim into the marketing frames — those headlines are specified in `BRIEF.md` §3.

## App Store name

**Streak Finder**

## Subtitle

Mine your Apple Health momentum

## Long description

Start building habits without starting from day one.

Streak Finder securely mines your Apple Health history to uncover the fitness streaks you've already built — and gives you the exact nudge you need to keep them alive.

There is nothing more motivating than realizing you're already on a winning streak. Whether you're closing your rings, hitting 10,000 steps, or prioritizing daily mindfulness, Streak Finder removes the friction of "starting over" by automatically tracking the momentum you already have.

**Key features**

- **Auto-discover your momentum.** Instantly find active streaks across 9+ Apple Health metrics: Steps, Exercise Minutes, Stand Hours, Active Energy, Workouts, Mindfulness, Sleep, Distance, and Flights Climbed.
- **Hero streaks & badges.** Put your most important goal front and center with the Hero Streak view, and collect badges as your habits grow.
- **Calendar heatmaps.** Visualize your progress over time. Every metric, every day, 30/90/180/365.
- **Widgets & complications.** Home Screen, Lock Screen, StandBy, and a native watchOS app with rich complications.
- **Smart reminders.** Optional 7 PM nudge if your Hero Streak is at risk of breaking that day.
- **Fiercely private.** Read-only HealthKit. 100% on-device. No accounts. No analytics. No network calls.

Don't break the chain.

## Voice cues

- Direct, confident, retro-arcade. The user is the player; the streak is the high score.
- No emojis. No "AI-powered." No "revolutionary." No "wellness journey."
- Verbs over adjectives. "Streaks you've already built." beats "Discover your effortless wellness streaks."
- Numbers welcome — "9 metrics", "10,114 steps", "30-day heatmap", "7 PM nudge".
- Never imply medical advice. Streak Finder *observes* Apple Health data — it doesn't coach or diagnose.
- Don't lean on "habit" or "routine" as primary vocab. Lead with "streak", "momentum", "the chain."
- The user is not failing — they just don't see the streak yet. Frame everything as discovery, not correction.
